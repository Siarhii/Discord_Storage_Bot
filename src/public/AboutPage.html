How It Works

  1. Upload your file
  2. Your file is encrypted with a password
  3. It gets split into bite-sized Discord-friendly chunks
  4. We'll graciously let Discord servers host your files
  5. Download whenever you want (patience recommended)

Note: You can specify a custom password during upload. If no password is provided, the default password "turd" will be used.

Pro Tips for the Technologically Challenged

  1. No,it does not have a "upload entire folder" button. For those of you who haven't discovered the wonders of compression, try zipping your folder first.
  2. Your file might take up to 30 minutes (or even more) to grace us with its presence on the webpage. It's almost as thrilling as watching paint dry, but with the added bonus of staring at a loading screen!
  3. For files over 100 MB (yes, It can handle those), you can get the download link via email.

Pro Tip: Staring at the loading screen is an excellent way to waste your time. 10/10 would recommend.

Things You Absolutely Must Know

  • It has rate limiters. So if you think spamming is cool, enjoy your 10-minute (or longer) timeout. You're welcome.
  • It's powered by a PC that's probably older than your little brother. Intel Core 2 Duo, vintage 2008. Feel free to complain about the speed; I'll pretend to care.
  • You want an account? heh. I've turned off new account creation because this mid PC can't handle your enthusiasm. If you absolutely must have an account, contact me directly. I'll consider your request... eventually.

Friendly Reminder

Don't make it your primary storage. Discord might decide to play 'delete everything' at any moment. Only upload stuff you're comfortable losing, like those "hilarious" memes that are funny (only to you).
